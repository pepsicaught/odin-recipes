Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

Serves 1(good portions)

  • Bowl of rice
  • 1/2 cup of kimchi
  • 1/2 cup of spam
  • 1/2 cup of green onion
  • 1/2 spoon of chilli powder
  • 1 spoon of soy sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Put kimchi in a pot and cut them in tiny pieces
  2. put oil on pan and put green onion in it
  3. as you can smell the onion, put chili powder
  4. stir until the kimchi is shiny
  5. put spam in the pan
  6. move the ingredients to the side and put soy sauce in the empty space
  7. when the soy sauce starts to bubble mix the ingredients
  8. put rice in the pan and stir every single rice
